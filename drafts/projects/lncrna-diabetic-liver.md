As **Project Associate I** at the CSIR-Institute of Genomics and Integrative
Biology (Delhi, 2024–2025), I led an ICMR-sponsored project on *lncRNA-mediated
gene regulation of the epigenetic machinery in the liver during diabetes*.

I investigated the role of long non-coding RNAs in regulating the DNA
methylation (DNMT) machinery under diabetic conditions, finding evidence of a
novel lncRNA regulatory axis in liver methylation. I designed and optimized the
experimental and computational workflows end to end — RNA–RNA interaction
analysis (IntaRNA, RNAHybrid, ViennaRNA), RNA-immunoprecipitation, and
sub-cellular fractionation assays — validating results by qRT-PCR in Hepa1-6 and
HepG2 cells. I conceived the experimental strategy, troubleshot protocols, and
interpreted results independently.
