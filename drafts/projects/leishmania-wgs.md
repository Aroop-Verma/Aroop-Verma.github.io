As **Research Assistant** at Tezpur University (Assam, 2023), I worked on an
ICMR-sponsored project integrating whole-genome sequencing with metabolic-pathway
analysis to find SNPs associated with drug resistance in *Leishmania donovani*.

I built end-to-end variant-discovery pipelines with GATK, bcftools, and snpEff —
covering QC, variant calling, and functional annotation — and correlated
mutations with the development of drug resistance in strains from the Indian
subcontinent. The work resulted in a peer-reviewed publication in *Antonie van
Leeuwenhoek* (2025).
